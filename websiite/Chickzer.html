About

Why Choose Us?

At chickzer, we’re all about good food and good vibes. From our saucy chicken fillet to our flavorful chao fan and snacks that always hit the spot — everything’s cooked fresh and served fast. No fancy talk, just food that makes you say, “ang sarap!” every time.

Testimonials

Chickzer started with a simple idea: to serve food that’s affordable, delicious, and never boring. What began as a small online food concept quickly became a crowd favorite — loved for its bold flavors, sulit meals, and consistent quality. We saw how people craved something new yet familiar — food that’s easy to love, easy to share, and always hits the spot. That’s exactly what Chickzer delivers every single day.
